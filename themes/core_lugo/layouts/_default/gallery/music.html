for the rest of your life - twikipedia

To me this album always feels like the summer of 2024. That time period was an exceptionally hard time for me due to a large change that was going on with my life. As a result, I will always have this album close to my heart.

This album is a large genre shift from laura's usual work (from rap to indie rock). I've been a fan of hers for a decently long time (since chronic?) and her artistic shift is extremely welcome. The mixing, vocals and lyrics all spoke to me heavily, creating emotions that I thought I had forgotten. I hope it does the same for you.

favourite track – far apart

Days Become Years - Cyborg9K

I'm not exactly sure why Cyborg9K's music has become associated with the worst people on the internet, especially since their song's messages have never been nihilistic or filled with dog whistles like the genre it's mostly grouped with. (the only apparent similarity being references to terminally online internet culture.)

Whatever the case, I've listened to Cyborg9K's music since 2023, and Days Become Years is their most memorable album. This is due to the album being a departure from most of their previous work, as it's far more melancholic both in style and lyrics.
