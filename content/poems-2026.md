## Wishing for a Great Snow

### By: Xiuhua Yu

I wish for a field of great and sudden snow, a snow thicker than death \
If it must suddenly come, then let it pour down and smash away all the hatred \
I need it to have exactly this power. Know that my insignificance is not a field of snow \
What an indifferent justification \
Let this detestable white collect on my body! In this boundless wasteland \
And let it erect an immortal gravestone \
For I am still of this filth: this vomit of curses \
and this flowing of blood. This shame-ignoring love, and those dovish questions \
Oh Snow, that great prophet, that hypocrite, that back-stabbing traitor \
Let it suffocate all the grass into a grave for me \
I have seen the snow's greatest benefit: \
One day, all the words that I failed to say can come from the depths of snow
